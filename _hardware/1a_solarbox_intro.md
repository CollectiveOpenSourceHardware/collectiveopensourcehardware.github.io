## Specifications:
- Maximal Voltage Input = **55 V**
- Maximal Power Input = **300 Wp**
- Capacity of Batteries = **920 Wh**
- DC Output Voltage = **12 V** and **5 V** (USB-power)
- Maximal Power Output = **600 W**
